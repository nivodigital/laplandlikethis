Who we are

We're not travel writers.
We just moved here.

Colin flew to Levi in May 2023 to remote work for a few weeks. His first time in Lapland. On day three he found a house being built and put an offer in — no mortgage, no residency plan, no idea if it would work.

Livi was at home in Cheltenham nursing one-year-old Finley when he called. She said "let's see." Keys in November 2023. Trial Christmas. Drove back for good in January 2024 with Hugo (7), Rowan (3) and Finley (1).

"Most Lapland content is written by people who visited once. We live here. That's the difference."
