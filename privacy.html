Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Lapland Like This · Levi, Finnish Lapland

The short version: we collect your email when you use the where-to-go tool and you opt in. We don't sell it, share it with third parties, or send you spam. You can unsubscribe any time. That's it.

Who we are

Lapland Like This is run by Colin and Livi, a family living in Levi, Finnish Lapland. We are not a travel agency, tour operator or commercial travel platform. We are a content-led travel resource with no affiliate relationships or commercial partnerships.

For privacy matters, contact us at: hello@laplandlikethis.com

What data we collect

We collect the following personal data when you use our where-to-go tool and choose to receive our newsletter:

  • Your first name (optional)
  • Your email address
  • Your where-to-go tool answers (destination preferences, travel origin, group type, budget range, season, priorities)

We also collect standard analytics data through our website hosting provider (page views, approximate location, device type). This data is anonymous and not linked to individuals.

How we use your data

We use the information you provide to:

  • Generate your personalised Lapland trip recommendations
  • Send you Lapland travel tips, seasonal guides and honest advice (only if you opt in)
  • Improve the accuracy of our where-to-go recommendations

We send 1–2 emails per month, only when we have something genuinely useful to say. We never send promotional emails on behalf of third parties.

Your rights

Under GDPR and UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Unsubscribe from our emails at any time (every email contains an unsubscribe link)
  • Object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@laplandlikethis.com and we will respond within 5 working days.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may use third-party tools to send emails (such as Mailchimp or similar) — these act as data processors on our behalf and are bound by appropriate data processing agreements.

Data retention

We retain your email address and name for as long as you remain subscribed to our newsletter. If you unsubscribe, we delete your personal data within 30 days. Where-to-go tool answers (without email) may be retained in anonymised, aggregated form to improve our recommendations.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies — only those necessary for the site to function correctly. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels or third-party analytics cookies that identify individuals.

Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this privacy policy, we will notify subscribers by email. The current version will always be available at this URL.

Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy or your personal data: hello@laplandlikethis.com
